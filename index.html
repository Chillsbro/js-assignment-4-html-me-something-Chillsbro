I'm Christian, Developer in training, Disc golfer, and Musician.

Showcasing my journey to becoming a professional developer, and my personal hobbies

Coding Music Disc Golf My Dog

My love for coding started when I moved to Kansas City from California.
I met a friend here, let's call him C-3P0. We met through a mutual friend.
We went disc golfing and C-3P0 brought up that he was working on a game.
I was intrigued that I could add sound effects with my music background,
and proceeded to show him a song of mine. He asked, "Who is this?" I said, "It's me!".

From there we collaborated for a bit with game design
and testing, and implementing sounds. Seeing how he would create physics,
movement, designs, and so much more through coding connected the wires in
my brain to finally see the connection between the logic and artistic side.
The fire was ignited, and ever since then I have set my goals on becoming
a professional developer and changing career paths to something I truly enjoy.

I started drumming at 12 years old, when my dad brought home an old electronic drum set.
I immediately sat down and started playing a simple beat. I had beatboxed a bit for fun,
so I think that helped me initially, funny enough. My dad is a concert pianist,
and had a band back in the 80's so we would frequently jam together and he would
help me with techniques, and dynamics. I taught myself from there on out,
played with a couple bands, and eventually started making music on my own.
It's been a blast, learning melody along with rythym, and envisioning journeys
to share through the medium of sound. For genres, as far as creating,
my favorites would be: Drum n' Bass, Chillstep, Electro, House, & Trance.
